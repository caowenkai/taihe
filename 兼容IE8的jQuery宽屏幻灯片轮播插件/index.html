In 2016, China has become the largest games market worldwide, with a revenue reaching 24.4 billion dollars and comprising a quarter of global market (Newzoo 2016). In 2017, the population of Chinese gamers will be reaching 565 million, almost double the size of the entire US population and is still growing (Newzoo 2017).
This is definitely a market developers shouldn’t ignore. This is also a market full of great opportunities and challenges. Over the years, Android has dominated Chinese mobile market; currently at 72% of the market (Digital Trends, 2015).In the West, we're used to getting our apps from either the Apple App Store or Google Play, but in China, where Android is prevalent, 81% of Android downloads are from the dozens of 3rd party markets, not the Google Play Store (TalkingData 2014).
Taihe Games was founded by a group of experts who have been working in Chinese mobile game publishing business for 5+ years. Our team members have led or participated in the iOS and Android publication business for the most successful foreign mobile games in China. In 2013 our CEO led a team to publish Plants vs. Zombies to Chinese Android market. Within a month after the launch day, PopCap/EA acquired 10 million Android players in China. Since then, our CEO Mr. Bokang Long and his team have published Rovio's Angry Birds, Supercell's Boom Beach, Clash of Clans and Clash Royale. Given great relationship with Android App stores, creative marketing planning, as well as high quality localization, player support and community service, he has made Supercell the most successful foreign mobile game company in China. Supercell's great success in China contributed to the valuation of 10.2 billion USD during the Supercell's acquisition by the Chinese internet giant, Tencent.
